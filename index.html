Thomas Seaman Company brings practical business expertise to complex litigation requiring the services of a Court-appointed neutral third party. These matters generally fall into three categories, insolvency, disputes, or regulatory matters. The function of a receiver is specialized and requires someone who understands being a fiduciary, is adept at operating in a judicial environment, but who also has the business skills to manage assets.
